# 👋 About ASRO

**ASRO** (AI Security Orchestration) is an enterprise-grade security dashboard that brings real-time vulnerability management and compliance tracking to your organization.

---

## 🎯 Our Mission

**Empower teams to manage security with clarity and speed.**

We believe security shouldn't be complicated. ASRO simplifies security operations by providing real-time visibility into vulnerabilities, compliance status, and security trends—all in one intuitive dashboard.

---

## 🌟 What We Do

### Security Dashboard
Real-time security metrics and vulnerability tracking powered by GitLab integration.

### Compliance Management
Track compliance across 6 major frameworks (SOC2, GDPR, OWASP, PCI-DSS, HIPAA, ISO 27001).

### Analytics & Insights
Historical trend analysis and deep insights into your security posture over time.

### AI-Driven Intelligence
Automated threat modeling and AI-powered recommendations for remediation.
